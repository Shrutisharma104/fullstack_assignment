Travelling is an investment in yourself

Kashmir is known for its natural beauty, which includes mountains, lakes, valleys, and gardens: Mountains: The Great Himalayan range and the Pir Panjal mountain range surround Kashmir.
Lakes: Kashmir has many lakes, including Dal Lake and Nageen Lake in Srinagar.
Valleys: Kashmir has many valleys, including Kishtwar, Markha, Suru, Shyok, Nubra, Nageen, Betaab, Dha Hanu, and Poonch.
Gardens: Kashmir has Mughal-era gardens and tulip gardens in Srinagar.
Flowers: Kashmir has hundreds of species of wildflowers in its alpine meadows. The tulip gardens in Srinagar are considered the largest tulip garden in Asia.
Trees: Kashmir has chinar tree-lined roads. In autumn, the trees take on scarlet, golden, and amber hues.
